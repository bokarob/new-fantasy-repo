The Balogunyom TK – Szanki OBSE match will be played on 18 October.

We’re starting the season the same way we finished the last one in Szuperliga: with postponed games! Everybody’s favorite, right? (Nope.) But at least it gives us a chance to remind you how postponed games affect your fantasy points.

Nothing has changed since last year, so if you remember the rules, you can skip ahead to the extra info below.

Here’s how it works:

  • Points from postponed games are calculated later, once the match is played.

  • If your players are involved, their points will be added to your team at that time.

  • This means your score may look temporarily lower, but once the postponed game is completed, your total points will be the same as if all matches had been played on schedule.

So in this case, if you have players from Balogunyom or Szank in Gameweek 1, their points will be added after October 18.
